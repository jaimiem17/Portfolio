2b. Reflection

I chose to use Green, blue, and red because they stand out the most apart from each other. I included yellow as lower than 1, but since that does not show up in the dataset, it did not really matter. The reason I chose to not use all 3 primary colors and use green instead was because the yellow did not show up well along the white. I also chose to not use the 'stroke' attribute because it made the colors less distinguishable from one another, even though this would have made the yellow an available color option. While I could've added axes, because I did not, this visualization lacks context. Another reason this visualization is less than ideal is the use of representing the ACT scores. Unless scaled on a larger range, it is very difficult to see difference in points. Another reason this visualization is less effective, is because there is no grid where you can see the actual values at the point, this makes it unreadable and can only really show the trend without giving any real data.
